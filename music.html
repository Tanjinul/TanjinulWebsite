Music I listen to!

Spring 2022

Collection of songs that was accumulated over new year and this play list will keep on growing till the end of my Spring semester, The Weekend and Gunna released album on the same day and it was a blast listening to both of the albums, some of my favorite from the Weekend album were Starry Eyes and Dont Break My Heart. Gunna had a fantastic album with a lot of good features. Some of my favorite ones were "you & me" with Chloe Bailey, "25k Jacket" with lil Baby and "so far ahead > empire"

Fall 2021

2021 Fall was pretty stacked with Kanye and Drake releasing, Pop Smoke also released an album over the summer. In the Fall it was mostly listens from these people with songs from Young Thug's PUNK. I thoroughly enjoyed music that was released over the Fall term. Some of my favorite's were Pop Smoke - 30 (feat. Bizzy Banks), Drake - 7am On Bridle Path, Baby Keem - family ties (with Kendrick Lamar) and Kanye West's - Ok Ok

Spring 2021

One of my favorite albums released on spring of 2021 and it was "Slime Language 2" by Young Stoner Life Records, this collaborative project had some of my favorite artist hop on the same track for songs. Lil Tjay and DJ Khaled also came out with albums but I think I listened to Slime Language 2 more than any album that season. I would tell someone to listen to the whole album because in my opinion there are no skips but some of my personal favorites were Ski (with Travis Scott), Trance (with Karlae and Yung Bleu). Some of my favorite from the DJ Khaled album was "Body in motion" and "This is my year" overall this was a season with a lot of great music that I enjoyed!
